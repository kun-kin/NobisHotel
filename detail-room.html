Nobis Suite

The Nobis Suite is a space that will offer you pure enjoyment. Step inside through the grand double doors and enjoy the amazing view overlooking the city of Copenhagen. The generous ceiling height breathes exclusivity, and the ambience of the room is enhanced by original details such as delicate cornices, hardwood oak floor in chevron pattern and window details. The interior and structure of the room bridges historical spirit and contemporary luxury. Materials such as marbles, wood, and glass, and a subtle color scale accentuated with beautiful blues makes the space embody superior quality.

The Nobis Suite offers plenty of room for both relaxation and social interaction. The heart of the suite is the comfortable sitting area with a spacious sofa and Swedese armchairs. The suite also holds a dining area seating up to 6 people suitable for casual meetings and private meals made to order from our own restaurant with Nordic influences.A solid wood custom-made cabinet separates the bedroom from the sitting room making it a tranquil place to enjoy a good night’s sleep. You sleep in a stately angular poster bed in steel with lush cotton sheets, ideal for those slow mornings when you want to enjoy a wonderful room service breakfast while watching the city come to life outside your window. The luxurious bathroom is tiled from floor to ceiling in beautiful grey Bardiglio marble and equipped with a spacious shower, a relaxing bathtub and separate toilet. The Nobis Suite also includes another separate toilet accessed from the entrance.
