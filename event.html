EVENT AT NOBIS HOTEL

Are you looking for a place with unique surroundings and beautiful designed interior?
Restaurant NOI is the perfect place for a festive dinner with delicious food or a cocktail reception with a vibrant atmosphere. We would love to help you arrange the venue of your dreams. Restaurant NOI can host 70 dinner guests and The Marble Bar have space for 30 guests. We also have three intimate rooms suitable for a private dining or other activities such as workshops or wine tasting. Restaurant NOI do also have a peaceful courtyard with dinner tables, comfortable lounge furniture and good music – brilliant for your next summer venue.

Read more about what we can offer or feel free to contact us for further information.

HYBRID MEETINGS & DIGITAL MEETINGS
