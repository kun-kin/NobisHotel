Nobis Hotel Copenhagen

With a prime location next to Tivoli Gardens, Copenhagen Central Station, City Hall Square and the internationally renowned Ny Carlsberg Glyptotek museum, you can easily enjoy the comfort of being within walking distance to all the delights that the capital has to offer. But you can also stay in and discover the Scandinavian architecture in our historic building from 1903.

The interior is designed in co-operation with the Swedish star architect Gert Wingårdh, to create our concept of contemporary five-star luxury - elegant and timeless, superior comfort with first-class service, but also personalized, sharp and updated in every detail.

CHRISTMAS CONCERT

Nobis Hotel recreates magical Christmas traditions and spreads Christmas joy. For the first time, Nobis invites you to Christmas carols performed by The Danish Girl Choir in beautiful surroundings.

We welcome you on November 6th at 17.15. The doors open at 16.45. Free entrance.

Feel free to find your preferred step on the staircase. There will also be an opportunity to buy a glass of bubbles or Gløgg (Mulled wine).

The concert ends at 18.00, hereafter you can enjoy dinner at Restaurant Noi or a glass of wine in The Marble Bar.

NEW YEAR'S EVE AT NOBIS

Gather your friends and loved ones and celebrate New Year’s Eve at Restaurant NOI.

Our head chef Mads Børgesen has created a festive 7-servings menu – infused with a secret ingredient and a spark of NOI.

Stay until midnight and jump into 2023 together with a glass of bubbles or a cocktail from The Marble Bar.
