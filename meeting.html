Meetings at Nobis Hotel

Nobis Hotel Copenhagen is offering three modern & stylish designed meeting rooms.
Two comfortably rooms with 10 seats and one with 14 seats – ideal for smaller meetings or hybrid meetings and intimate gatherings. All three rooms are highly functional and equipped with a TV-screen, high-speed Wi-Fi, climate control and an old-fashioned whiteboard. The rooms are minimalistic decorated with spectacular flooring of massive Douglas fir by Dinesen and comfortable design chairs by Wegner.

We love to make your day more effective and stress-free. Our lovely staff are always at your disposal and ready to serve a wide selection of beverages and delicious snacks. We will also be delighted to assist booking accommodation with us at Nobis or calling a taxi.

Excite your guests or colleagues with a tasteful food experience. Take a break and enjoy a lunch or dinner in our restaurant NOI Copenhagen or dine in the meeting room. NOI represents the European kitchen – serving honest food created with carefully selected ingredients.

HYBRID MEETINGS & DIGITAL MEETINGS

Of course, we offer good technology to facilitate hybrid- and digital meetings. The price for a hybrid meeting depends on the accessories needed. Please let us know in advance which accessories you need.
