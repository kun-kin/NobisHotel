NOI

Nobis Hotel Copenhagen proudly presents restaurant NOI. The team behind NOI creates an inspiring menu with influence from all over Europe. With local ingredients and simplicity the chefs works as an orchestra in the old building of the Royal Danish Academy of Music located in the heart of Copenhagen. Enjoy a symphony of delicious courses and a glass of our carefully selected wines, in an elegant and vibrant atmosphere designed by the Swedish architect company Wingårdhs.
